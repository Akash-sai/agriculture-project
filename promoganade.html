POMOGRANATE:-
1.Site and Variety Selection:Choose a sunny location with well-draining soil. Select a pomegranate variety that suits your climate and purpose.

2.Planting:Plant pomegranate trees in well-prepared soil during the spring or fall. Ensure proper spacing and water the young plant well.

3.Watering and Care:Provide consistent watering, especially during the first few years. Once established, water moderately, and apply a balanced fertilizer in spring and early summer.

4.Pruning:Prune the tree regularly to shape it, remove dead wood, and encourage healthy growth. Pomegranates respond well to pruning.

5.Harvesting:Harvest the pomegranates when they have developed a deep color and sound metallic when tapped. Timing may vary based on the specificÂ variety.
